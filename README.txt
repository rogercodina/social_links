Social Links
============

Creates a block with social links. 

After installation:

1) Go to admin/config/system/settings to configure social URLs for your site.
2) Assign the permission "Configure social links" to roles you will grant access to previous configuration page.
3) Go to admin/structure/block and move block "Social links block" to desired theme region


This module was downloaded from https://github.com/rogercodina/social_links
